Nice to meet you! A little more about me...
Born and raised in New York, I am a designer studying Information Science with a minor in Game Design at Cornell University.
Immediately in college, I realized that creating ubiquitous applications and curating unforgettable design experiences are paths I want to pursue after taking a student-run Digital Product Design course.
Through my background in fine arts, I hope to combine the emotional aspects of visual design with the human psychology and ergonomics of product design. I aspire to empower people through my designs and create work that is easily accessible to my community.
Feel free to shoot me a message at rs2279@cornell.edu if you would like to work together.
